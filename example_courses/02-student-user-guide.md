# Welcome to Toolspaedeia

Toolspaedeia is designed to be your central hub for academic learning. Whether you are studying programming, mathematics, or literature, this platform provides a structured way to master complex subjects.

## Setting Up Your Account
To get started, create your account and complete your profile. This allows the platform to suggest courses based on your current skill level.

## Navigating the Dashboard
The dashboard is divided into three main sections:
- **My Courses**: Where you can track your progress in enrolled courses.
- **Course Catalog**: A library of available academic paths.
- **Resources**: Quick access to documentation and external tools.

## User Interface Overview
The interface utilizes a clean, distraction-free layout. Use the sidebar to switch between modules and the top navigation bar to access your profile settings.

# Managing Your Learning Journey

Effective learning requires a plan. Toolspaedeia helps you organize your studies through **Learning Paths**.

## Setting Learning Goals
Within each course, you can set weekly goals. For example:
- Complete 2 modules per week.
- Score 80% or higher on all quizzes.

## Understanding Prerequisites
Some advanced courses require you to complete foundational courses first. These are marked with a 🔒 icon. You must pass the final assessment of the prerequisite course to unlock the next level.

## Tracking Progress
The progress bar at the top of each module shows your completion percentage. A module is considered "Complete" only after the content has been read and the quiz has been passed.
